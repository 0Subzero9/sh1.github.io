Evanescent

-Risal (3065)

Chuckles at a bench,
Students in the playground,
All just a memory now.

Pen clicks of a new year begins,
Calendar changes so fast,
Those alphabets in kindergarten,
Where have I them lost?

That white uniform, schoolbag,
Shoes soaked in mud,
Rainy days with football matches,
Can you call them absurd?

Slowly passing by,
Similarly out of sight,
Losing touch with my,
Juvenile friends of high.

Will I ever meet them,
Or is this the end?
I guess we will know,
If the curse is evanescent.
