Incompetence

-Sheikh (3216)

With the withering rose held in my hand,
Smiling, by the window I stand.
Funny how life feels so light,
For now, it is a mess, nothing is right.

Just like the rose I wanted to be,
For the epitome of perfection,
Was what it had come to be.
Amidst this I forgot to care
For the feelings of my head.
I fell into this large mess,
As my mind blended with sense.

But then I understood,
Eternal nor internal beauty is real,
As such people aren’t perfect.
Souls get smashed,
Words fall in the dark,
When someone dislikes me for themselves
What’s the point of being myself?
Society is something I can’t deal with,
They will find fault with me for everything.
